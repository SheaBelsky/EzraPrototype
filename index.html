Think big. Dream big. Create big.

Ezra is a revolutionary application for the Science Olympiad community to engage current and potential olympians to reach their greatest potential, and help teachers, proctors, and event managers organize the best event possible.

Learn more »

FUNCTIONAL & INNOVATIVE GRADING

Take the hassle out of grading your events. Science is always changing, why shouldn't the way you grade be the same way? For test events, automatically score and rank schools after their points are input. In build events, Ezra will automatically tier schools and allow for grading based on rubric components; points will be calculated automatically, versus needing to be calculated beforehand.

LEARN WITH EZRA DISCOVER

Learn the details, intricacies, and fundamentals of every Science Olympiad event with in-depth video classes, lectures, and guides. These videos are free for everyone to watch, and they always will be.

REGISTRATION, LOGISTICS, & VOLUNTEER MANAGEMENT

Running a tournament is easy. Follow all teams from the moment they register through the awards ceremony. Ezra will intuitively organize registration data for teams, remember information on old teams, allow electronic submission of forms, notify coaches of registration problems or missing items, coordinate volunteers, proctors, and on-site personnel so you have a bird's eye of the entire event no matter where you are.
